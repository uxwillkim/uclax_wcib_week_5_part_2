Welcome to Make-A-Story!

Ever felt the need to write your own stories? To become a world-renowned author of a best seller? Well, this isn't the place for that, but it is a place to express your creative juices and come up with your own silly story. With the help of the StoryMaker2000, you just make a few selections and watch your creation come to life. Give it a try!

Step 1: Name

Start with a name, any name. Could be your own, your hamster's name, or the name of that one guy who lives down the street.

Step 2: Measurement

Metric or Imperial, or even InterGalactic. Pick your poison.

Step 3: Vacation

What's your idea of the perfect getaway spot? Choose and see where the story takes you.
